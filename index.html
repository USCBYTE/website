Our Purpose

BYTE aims to inspire Junior High students to pursue higher education in STEAM fields and become developers and educated consumers.

Secondly, BYTE aims to educate students about open source alternatives to traditional software in order to make technology more accessible.

Thirdly, BYTE aims to inform students of the creative opportunities within the technology and computer science fields.
